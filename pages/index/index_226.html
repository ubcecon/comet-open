Making Sense of Economic Data (ECON 226)

This section contains material to support UBC’s ECON 226 Making Sense of Economic Data. This course is an introductory course intended to give students a general introduction to probability and statistics in an econometric setting.

  • These modules in particular are designed to be used in 1 or 2 one-hour long lab environments, with TA or Instructor supervision
  • They can also be used for self-study, with some additional effort

These materials are a review of basic probability, which contains overlap with AP and IB Statistics from high school.
